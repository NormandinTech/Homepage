About

One founder.
Sixteen products.
Five verticals.

NormandinTECH is a solo-founded SaaS company based in Paradise, California — a town destroyed by the 2018 Camp Fire. That event directly inspired GridIQ, built to give rural electric cooperatives the grid intelligence tools that could have prevented it.

Every product is built iteratively with AI-assisted development, deployed on the same battle-tested infrastructure stack — DigitalOcean, PM2, nginx, Let's Encrypt — and monetized through Gumroad and Stripe.

Registered on SAM.gov for federal procurement. Available for enterprise partnerships, investor conversations, and white-label licensing across all verticals.
