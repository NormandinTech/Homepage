// Founder
Seth Normandin
SOLO DEVELOPER & FOUNDER
Paradise, CA 95969

On November 8, 2018, a PG&E transmission line failed and the Camp Fire destroyed Paradise, California. 85 people died. It was the deadliest wildfire in state history.


I watched it happen. I stayed. I rebuilt — and I built GridIQ specifically so it doesn't happen again. Every feature in the platform traces back to the exact failure modes that destroyed this town.


NormandinTECH is a solo software operation. No VC money. No team. Just code and conviction — from a town that the utility industry burned down.
